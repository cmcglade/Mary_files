The Canadian Icons Project

The Project

A photographic portfolio celebrating 150 of Canadian Icons. These beautiful portraits seek to reveal intimate moments with 150 Canadians who have excelled in the arts, sciences, sports and academia.

About Don

Don Dixon is a Toronto-based photographer who is passionate about taking beautiful portrait photos. His work is known throughout the US and Canada. Browse his portfolio for this project and others.

Donations

The Canadian Icons Project is currently accepting both private and corporate donations. Please click her if you'd like to contribute to our work.
