The Canadian Icons Project

What Is the Canadian Icons Project?

The Canadian Icons Project is the passion-project of Toronto-based Photographer, Don Dixon. It is a celebration of 150 years of Canadian excellence, in the arts, sciences, academia and beyond.

About Don

Don Dixon is an established photographer, based in Toronto, but with work that spans across Canada and the United States. Don's utmost passion is in portrait photography, as reflected in the Icons Project.
